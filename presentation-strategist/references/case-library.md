# Case Library

Use these cases as calibration references, not templates to copy.

## Strategic Sales And Category Creation

### Zuora Sales Narrative

Lesson: sell the customer's changed world before selling the product.

Calibration:

- Ask what changed in the customer's world.
- Identify the cost of staying with the old operating model.
- Make product the path, not the hero.

### Drift Strategic Pitch

Lesson: name a new buyer expectation and force urgency.

Calibration:

- Identify the old game and new game.
- Account for sales/marketing stakeholder split.

### Salesforce "No Software"

Lesson: the enemy can be an operating model.

Calibration:

- Define the old burden clearly.
- Convert technical delivery into business agility.

### HubSpot Inbound Marketing

Lesson: category creation often requires teaching before selling.

Calibration:

- Include education if the buyer does not yet name the problem.

## Fundraising And Early Company Pitches

### Airbnb Early Pitch

Lesson: prove behavior before grand market size.

Calibration:

- Make strange behavior feel concrete and validated.
- Use simple problem, transaction logic, and market entry.

### UberCab Early Pitch

Lesson: anchor an unfamiliar model in familiar pain.

Calibration:

- Show constrained beachhead before large scale.
- Address operational and regulatory risk.

### LinkedIn Series B

Lesson: when present metrics are weak, prior promises kept and concept logic can carry trust.

Calibration:

- Ask what proof exists besides revenue.
- Make founder credibility explicit if it matters.

### Buffer Seed Deck

Lesson: clean traction can beat grand narrative.

Calibration:

- Lead with traction when evidence is simple and strong.

### Sequoia Business Plan Framework

Lesson: investors repeatedly ask purpose, problem, why now, market, product, team, financials.

Calibration:

- Use as checklist, not universal slide order.

### YC Demo Day Guidance

Lesson: field constraints force ruthless message selection.

Calibration:

- In short pitches, cut anything that does not move investor belief.

### Toutiao 2013 Business Plan

Lesson: technical advantage must become user behavior, growth, and market potential.

Calibration:

- Translate algorithms or technology into retention, engagement, and scale.

## IPO And Mature Investor Communication

### Alibaba IPO Roadshow

Lesson: turn a complex ecosystem into an investable story.

Calibration:

- Build a simple mental model before segment detail.
- De-risk scale, governance, and market context.

### Xiaomi IPO / Ecosystem Story

Lesson: ecosystem businesses must connect devices, users, services, and margins.

Calibration:

- Force the business model bridge when categories overlap.

### NVIDIA Investor Presentations

Lesson: business reviews need both metric truth and durable market thesis.

Calibration:

- Bind segment numbers to a market narrative.
- Surface supply, demand, and cycle risks.

### Microsoft AI Annual Narrative

Lesson: incumbents must explain why a platform shift strengthens them.

Calibration:

- Address "why won't this shift hurt you?" directly.

### Stripe Annual Letters

Lesson: company progress can be framed through ecosystem progress.

Calibration:

- Use ecosystem evidence when the company's product depends on broader adoption.

### Amazon Shareholder Letters

Lesson: repeated operating principles can become an investor trust contract.

Calibration:

- Connect current facts to long-term principles and prior commitments.

## Product Launch And Category Redefinition

### Apple iPhone 2007

Lesson: category redefinition can recombine familiar objects into a new mental model.

Calibration:

- Decide whether the task is product introduction or category redefinition.
- Use demo as proof.

### Tesla Secret Master Plan

Lesson: current constraints can be justified by a staged future path.

Calibration:

- Connect today's limitation to tomorrow's strategy.

### Xiaomi SU7 Launch

Lesson: cross-category launches must first overcome trust gaps.

Calibration:

- Lead with credibility, product proof, ecosystem meaning, and adoption risk reduction.

### OpenAI DevDay 2023

Lesson: developer launches need demos, lower adoption friction, and a clear capability map.

Calibration:

- Show how the audience can build or adopt immediately.

## Internal Alignment And Decision Systems

### Netflix Culture Deck / Memo

Lesson: culture communication defines real tradeoffs, not slogans.

Calibration:

- Ask what the organization accepts and rejects.
- Expect psychological resistance.

### Amazon Six-Page Narratives

Lesson: better decisions may require written reasoning instead of slides.

Calibration:

- Recommend memo when complexity or decision risk is high.

### Amazon PR/FAQ

Lesson: start from future customer outcome, then force objections before building.

Calibration:

- Use when product value or customer problem is unclear.

## Crisis, Risk, And Failure

### NASA Columbia PowerPoint Failure

Lesson: slide compression can bury uncertainty and weaken judgment.

Calibration:

- For risk briefings, recommend memo/appendix and explicit uncertainty.

### Johnson & Johnson Tylenol Crisis

Lesson: ethical action is stronger proof than messaging.

Calibration:

- In crisis, lead with harm, responsibility, action, and monitoring.

### Toyota 2010 Recall

Lesson: defensive framing worsens trust when accountability is expected.

Calibration:

- Answer trust damage before defending the organization.
